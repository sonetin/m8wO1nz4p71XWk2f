submit: label: Create my private social network href: index.html?plan=premium+ faq: - id: free question: A free network? body: |

Your private social network is free to use with unlimited users and groups. Invite as contacts as you want and post as much posts as you wish.

Your network posts can be accessed within 6 months per default. This can be extended to much more.

Upgrade the data retention period to access oldr posts and files, either by unlocking the feature with points or by buying it (coming soon).

- id: support question: What about support? body: |

Free networks and Premium networks can use a dedicated community space.

Premium+ networks benefit from a VIP support during open hours.

- id: customization question: Customizations? body: |

Your network is yours. This means that _you_ decide of the name and logo of your community. Even in the Free plan.

In the Free plan, SonetIN promotion is enabled. This is us bragging a little bit about our services to your users, both tenderly and painlessly.

You can disabled it by switching to the Premium plan and benefit from a complete white-label network.
